Gallery

Visiting Hasta Shilpa Heritage Village in Manipal was like stepping into history, with its beautifully restored traditional homes from various regions of India. Each structure, from Mangalore tile-roofed houses to the grand Chettinad mansion, showcased intricate craftsmanship. The knowledgeable guides enriched the experience, sharing the stories behind the homes and artifacts. The peaceful surroundings made it an unforgettable journey through India's architectural heritage, leaving us with a deep appreciation for the effort to preserve these cultural treasures.
